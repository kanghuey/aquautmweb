Coach Low Xue Ting

A former competitive swimmer from Johor who won gold in ASEAN School Games. She leveraged her athletic experience into founding a swim academy called EZY Swimming Academy in 2013, with the vision to raise swimming standards in Johor, offering programmes from beginner to elite, and achieving strong results both in volume (thousands of students) and performance (state and national level).
