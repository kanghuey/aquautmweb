About AquaUTM

The UTM Swimming Pool was completed in 2011 and officially began operating in 2012. It features two pools with different depths — 1.35 meters and 5 meters — catering to both beginners and professional swimmers. The facility also includes a grandstand area that can accommodate up to 1,000 spectators, making it an ideal venue for various aquatic events and activities.

A wide range of programs can be held at the UTM Swimming Pool, including scuba diving license training, dinner events, seminars, swimming competitions, aqua Zumba sessions, and more. With its excellent facilities and vibrant atmosphere, the UTM Swimming Pool continues to serve as a hub for water sports and community engagement at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia
